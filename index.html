Hi there, I'm Sean Metzger!

I'm a first year Ph.D. student in the UC Berkeley/UCSF graduate program in bioengineering advised by Eddie Chang.

I am interested in modeling neural population dynamics, building a brain computer interface to restore speech, and learning as much as I can about deep learning and machine leearning. Before starting my Ph.D. I did a B.S and M.S in Electrical Engineering at Stanford, where I did research with Daniel Palanker on retinal prostheses and Bill Dally on high efficiency power conversion.
